Printer:

We use AnkerMaker M5C. But any FDM 3D printer will work.


Software:

I recommend Prusa MK3S+ with Prusa Slicer, choose the correct software based on your printer.


Settings (PLA):
  • 50% Infill, supports, 0.1mm Quality, Filament Type: AnkerMaker PLA+.
  • 220C Nozzle and 70C Bed

    • Settings (TPU):
  • 20% Infill, supports, 0.1mm Quality, Filament Type: FLASHFORGE TPU 95A, Set the all printing speed as 15mm/s in setting.
  • 230C Nozzle and 65C Bed
