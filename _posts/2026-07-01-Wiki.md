I am pleased to share that my scientific photograph, “Smoked air-flow of modified Bernoulli-based gripper for grasping textile materials,” was selected as a finalist in the Wiki Science Competition 2025.
<br/><img src='/images/Jet_gripper_air-flow.jpg' width='300'>

The image illustrates the airflow-modified Bernoulli grippers designed to handle textile materials. The proposed designs reduce turbulence-induced vibrations by preventing or redirecting airflow away from the gripper–material interaction zone.
